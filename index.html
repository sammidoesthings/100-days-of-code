SammiDoesThings does #100DaysOfCode

Who is SammiDoesThings?

Sammi is a new web developer, Minecraft streamer, and LEGO collector. Plagued by the horribly conflicting toxins of procrastination and perfectionism, she has a habit of planning or beginning projects without ever fleshing them out or finishing them, which results in a whole lot of not actually creating things. This is her 2021 attempt to fight that, and make progress every day.

What is #100daysofcode?

#100daysofcode is an online coding event that challenges participants to code for one hour per day and to expand their technical network. Sammi's version of the challenge keeps the core purpose of the challenge in tact (code for one hour every day) but alters the network expansion requirements by challenging herself to connect with at least one person a week through a tech event or challenge, grab a coffee (in-person/virtual) with someone she wouldn't normally, or collaborating with someone on a project outside her comfort zone.
